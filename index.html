About Me

I am Saravanan Ganesh! I love teaching coding to young aspiring programmers along with moral values. I live in Bangalore - The Silicon Valley of India. I am proud to be a teacher and a coach.

I completed my engineering from Bangalore College of Engineering in the year 2007 and got placed in TARGET CORPORATION. Fortunately, Target chose to outsource their fresher training to Mysore Infosys and we were there in Mysore Infosys for a period of 6 months, where the world's best training was given to us. We were just 10 of us from TARGET among 5000 Infosians.

Post the fresher's training I worked in Point Of Sale Team in TARGET CORPORATION - Bangalore for over a period of 7 years at various levels. Finally on 3rd January 2014 I quit my dream job to pursue another dream of creating and nurturing world class programmers and founded TechSparx Technology Training School. I established a Training Center in AECS Layout, Kundalahalli Bangalore.
